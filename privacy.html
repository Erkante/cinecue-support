Privacy Policy for CineCue

Effective date: April 2, 2025

Thank you for choosing CineCue. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about how we handle user data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

Data Collection and Use

Advertising

CineCue uses third-party advertising services (like Google AdMob) to display advertisements within the app. These third-party services may collect anonymous information about your device, such as your device ID, advertising ID, or IP address, to deliver relevant advertisements.

Analytics

We use third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics for Firebase) to understand how our users engage with CineCue. These analytics tools collect information such as session duration, pages visited within the app, interaction patterns, and other general usage data. This information is anonymized and aggregated, and is used solely for improving the performance and user experience of the application.

Personal Data

CineCue does not collect personally identifiable information (PII). There is no user registration or account creation, and we do not request or store sensitive personal details such as names, email addresses, or passwords.

Device Information

We may collect non-personal device-specific information such as your device type, operating system version, and app version to optimize performance and troubleshoot technical issues.

Third-party Services

The third-party services used by CineCue have their own privacy policies. Please consult these policies for detailed information:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/policies/analytics

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data collected through our app against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the CineCue application and on the app's listing in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. Users are advised to periodically review this Privacy Policy for any changes.
